About Bansuk Dental Clinic

Specialist in Dental Conservation and Endodontics

A Dental Conservation Specialist, often referred to as an Endodontist, focuses on preserving natural teeth through advanced techniques aimed at maintaining the integrity and health of the tooth structure. Unlike general dentists who provide a broad range of dental care, or other dental specialists who may focus on surgical or cosmetic procedures, a dental conservation specialist is dedicated to saving and restoring natural teeth, even in complex cases.

Advanced Expertise in Saving Natural Teeth

With years of specialized training beyond general dentistry, conservation specialists use state-of-the-art techniques, such as root canal therapy, to treat damaged or infected teeth and prevent extractions. Their advanced expertise allows them to address deep-rooted dental issues, preserving teeth that might otherwise be removed.

Focus on Long-Term Dental Health and Minimally Invasive Procedures

Endodontists prioritize long-term dental health by employing minimally invasive procedures that focus on the natural structure of each tooth. This approach minimizes patient discomfort and ensures that treatments are designed for durability and longevity, protecting the original teeth whenever possible

Precision and Pain Management for Complex Cases

A dental conservation specialist is skilled in handling complex cases involving the tooth's inner structures (such as the pulp and nerves), often using advanced imaging technologies for accurate diagnosis and treatment. With a keen focus on patient comfort, they are experts in pain management, making the process as seamless as possible.

Advanced Dental Equipment at Bansuk Dental Restoration Clinic

1. Dentsply Sirona Intego Treatment Chair for Maximum Comfort

Our clinic is equipped with the premium Intego Treatment Chair by Dentsply Sirona, designed for exceptional patient comfort during procedures. This high-end chair offers ergonomic support, smooth adjustment controls, and a relaxing experience, allowing patients to feel at ease even during longer treatments.

2. Zeiss EXTARO 300 Dental Microscope for Precision in Complex Treatments

For high-precision endodontic treatments, we utilize the Zeiss EXTARO 300 dental microscope. This advanced microscope allows our specialists to see intricate details inside the root canals, enabling precise and effective treatment even in complex cases. With EXTARO 300, we achieve enhanced accuracy and better long-term outcomes in root canal procedures.

3. Digital X-Ray for Safe and Accurate Imaging

Our digital X-ray technology provides high-resolution images with significantly reduced radiation exposure compared to conventional X-rays. This allows us to diagnose and monitor dental conditions accurately while prioritizing patient safety.

4. 3D CT Scan for Detailed Diagnosis and Treatment Planning

We use a 3D CT scanner that generates comprehensive, three-dimensional images of your teeth, jawbone, and surrounding structures. This technology is essential for planning complex procedures, such as implant placement and bone grafting, ensuring accurate treatment and optimal results.

5. Intraoral Camera for Clear Patient Communication

Our intraoral camera allows for a close-up view of your mouth, enabling our team to show you detailed images of your dental condition. This helps us explain your treatment options clearly, making sure you are informed and confident throughout your dental journey.

6. Ultrasonic Scaler for Efficient and Comfortable Teeth Cleaning

Our ultrasonic scaler gently removes tartar and plaque from your teeth using ultrasonic vibrations. It provides a deep clean below the gum line, supporting gum health and leaving your teeth feeling refreshed after each visit
