Dental Treatment Costs for Foreign Residents in Korea

If you are a foreign resident in Korea and enrolled in the National Health Insurance (NHI), you can benefit from coverage for certain basic dental treatments. Below is an overview of what is covered and what is not:

ðŸ¦· Treatments Covered by NHI

  • Root Canal Therapy: Essential treatment to save a damaged or infected tooth.
  • Periodontal Treatment: Care for gum diseases, such as scaling or deep cleaning.

ðŸš« Treatments Not Covered by NHI

  • Dental Implants: Advanced tooth replacement procedure.
  • Crowns and Bridges: Restorative solutions for damaged or missing teeth.
  • Resin Fillings: Tooth-colored fillings for cavities or minor restorations.
  • Aesthetic Dentistry: Cosmetic procedures such as whitening and veneers.

For treatments not covered by NHI, the costs will vary depending on the complexity of the procedure and materials used. At Bansuk Dental Clinic, we provide clear and detailed estimates before starting any treatment.
