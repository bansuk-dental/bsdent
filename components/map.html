Find Us: Conveniently Located in Central Seoul

Bansuk Dental Restoration Clinic is located in the heart of Seoul, making it easily accessible for both residents and visitors.

Address

19, Mugyo-ro, Jung-gu, Seoul, Republic of Korea

How to Get Here

  • By Subway: Take Line 1 or Line 2 and get off at City Hall Station, Exit 3. The clinic is a short 5-minute walk from the exit.
  • By Bus: Use the Mugyo-ro Bus Stop, served by multiple routes, and the clinic is just a few steps away.
  • By Car: Parking is available at The Exchange Seoul building, where our clinic is located

Landmarks Nearby

We are conveniently located near popular landmarks such as Seoul City Hall and Cheonggyecheon Stream, making it easy to find our clinic. You'll find a Starbucks on the first floor of The Exchange Seoul building, we are at the side of the building.
