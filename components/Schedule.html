Treatment Timeline Guide for Visitors to Korea

If you're staying in Korea for a limited time, we've organized our treatments based on the time required to complete them. This helps you plan your dental care efficiently during your stay.

1-Day Procedures

  • Direct Filling:

    Quick restoration for cavities or small defects.

  • Tooth Cleaning:

    Scaling and polishing for a fresher smile.

  • Whitening:

    Brighten your teeth in just one session.

  • Tooth Extraction:

    Simple extractions done promptly.

2-3 Visits (Within 1 Week)

  • Inlay or Onlay:

    Custom restorations for moderate damage.

  • Crown:

    Custom-made tooth caps for damaged teeth.

  • Periodontal Treatment:

    Deep cleaning for gum health.

  • TMJ Splint:

    Relief for jaw pain and tension.

3-4 Visits (Within 2 Weeks)

  • Comprehensive treatments requiring additional time:

  • Root Canal Therapy & Crown:

    Restoring severely damaged teeth.

  • Implant Surgery:

    Surgical placement of implants (crown placement after 3 months).

At Bansuk Dental Clinic, we strive to accommodate your schedule while ensuring high-quality care.
